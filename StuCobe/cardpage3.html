Yuvaan

Literary and Film Society

Yuvaan, the official literary fest of Delhi Technological University, laid the foundation of a legacy with its premier edition in 2017. Deriving from the Hindi word Yuva, the fest captures the vigour, boldness and the spontaneity associated with the youth. It represents the quintessential voice responsible for moulding the future. Yuvaan exhibits the perfect amalgamation of technology, science and literature. The fest emphasises the literary aspects including and not restricted to writing, reading and observing prose and poetry. The aim is to provide entertainment, enlightenment, and instruction to the audience. It is often said, “Actions speak louder than words”; Yuvaan proves to be magnetic by pulling every person out there powered by the fusion of classical literature with modern, divergent and unconventional literary events. Yuvaan'17, had a humble beginning, and was followed by many interesting events such as talk show, poets collective, motivational speakers, bonfire night etc. and the closing ceremony. It also included conventional events like poetry slam, conventional debate, letter writing etc. Yuvaan'18 was a bigger hit. It created a furore in the Delhi NCR region. With the theme 'The Elixir of Life,'​ over 40k people participated and attended Yuvaan. Yuvaan has been graced by DTU's own alumni and national bestseller, Durjoy Datta, artists of the viral slam poetry group UnErase Poetry, the wonderful Nizami Brothers who gave the perfect closing ceremony in the form of a Sufi Night, Major General GD Bakshi, celebrated journalists such as Sunetra Chaudhary, Rohit Sardana, Sanket Upadhyay and many more. Yuvaan has set high bars and is ready to become the biggest college literary fests in India.
